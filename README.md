# Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard — Power BI

### Project Overview:
#
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing global Airbnb listing and review data across 10 major cities, covering the platform's full lifecycle from its launch in 2008 through the COVID-19 pandemic impact in 2020.
### Dataset:
#
Source: Maven Analytics Data Playground

Records: 2,79,712 Listings | 5,373K Reviews | 1,82,024 Hosts | 144 Property Types
### Dashboard Pages & Key Insights:
#
1. New Listings Growth (2008–2020)
- Airbnb's lifecycle mapped across 6 phases: Introduction → Growth → Maturity → Decline → Reinvention → COVID-19
- Peak new listings recorded in 2015
- Growth slowed due to local regulation tightening in 2016–17
- Became profitable in second half of 2016
- COVID-19 brought listings to near zero in 2020
2. Market Share, Pricing & Ratings by City
- Paris, NYC & Sydney account for ~50% of all listings and 59% of total reviews
- Average prices: Hotel Room ($800) > Entire Place ($673) > Shared Room ($580) > Private Room ($462)
- Mexico City & Rio de Janeiro are the highest-rated cities overall
- Hong Kong & Istanbul score the lowest, especially in Cleanliness and Value
3. Seasonality, Review Frequency & Host Trust
- 86.5% of customers left only one review; 98.8% reviewed 3 times or less
- Paris & Rome dominate reviews from April to August (European summer travel)
- New York peaks in November–December (holiday season)
- 66.9% of hosts are identity-verified with a profile picture
